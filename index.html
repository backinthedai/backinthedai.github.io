Welcome to BITD (back in the dai)

For the last 10+ years I have been working, maintaining and building servers, networks and global IT teams. Started coding as a recreational hobby that has turned into a passion. The enjoyment of figuring out algorithms, teaching other peers or choosing the right color of a CSS stlying has sometimes kept me up all night. Below are some of the projects I have completed and am very proud of. I left the projects as is when it was completed to show myself that we all started out from messy codes. Enjoy!

Currently I am mainly focusing on JavaScript, HTML and CSS. However, I am looking forward to C#, .NET framework, and some Python. So much to learn so little time...
